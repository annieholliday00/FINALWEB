welcome

this is a pretentious little website made to exhibit my art and 3d models, which i've also filled with random quotes and weird design choices to spice things up a little.

the first background is a sketch by tim sale, an artist who's worked on comics like 'long halloween' and 'dark victory'. i particularly love his scarecrow design, which is why i decided to toss him on the front page! the second background is from the 'zaba' album for glass animals, created by Micah Lidberg. It was only fitting to put Glass animals as my first musical artist on my javascript player thingy.

contrary to my spectacular website design, i dont have much experience with css or javascript. i did used to do html layouts back in 2012/2013, which is definitely my strong suit, but i even struggled to keep up with that and make this work effectively.
