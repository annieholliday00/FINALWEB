welcome

this is a little website made to exhibit my art and 3d models, which i've also filled with random quotes and weird design choices to spice things up a little.

the first background is a sketch by tim sale, an artist who's worked on comics like 'long halloween' and 'dark victory'. i particularly love his scarecrow design, which is why i decided to toss him on the front page! the second background is from the 'zaba' album for glass animals, created by Micah Lidberg. It was only fitting to put Glass animals as my first musical artist on my javascript player thingy, which was the hardest thing to implement on here, other than the 3d model previewer.

if you want to use the music player, you MIGHT want to turn your volume down a bit first. I have four songs on there at the moment but I couldn't figure out how to work backwards through the list, so it's basically just a roundabout. ALSO the songs repeat themselves when they're done, and you might want to click twice on the skip button for the first time because it doesn't like to listen to you.
